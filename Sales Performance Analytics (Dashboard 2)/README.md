**Tool:** Power BI

**Dashboard Focus:** Revenue Performance, Sales Volume, Profitability, Channel Mix, and Geographic Distribution

## Overview

The **Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive commercial view of sales performance across a global e-commerce business model inspired by Amazon. Built using a synthetic dataset of 100,000 transaction-level records, this dashboard focuses on:

- Monitoring total revenue and order performance  
- Evaluating sales growth trends over time  
- Analyzing product category contribution  
- Understanding channel-level revenue distribution  
- Assessing geographic sales concentration  
- Tracking profitability and discount behavior  

The dashboard is designed for sales leadership, commercial strategy teams, category managers, and executive stakeholders to obtain a clear, interactive, and data-driven understanding of sales performance.

By combining executive KPI cards, trend analysis, category breakdowns, geographic visualization, and channel distribution insights, the dashboard delivers a holistic commercial performance overview.

---

## Business Storyline & Analytical Flow

### 1. Executive Sales Snapshot – KPI Cards

The dashboard opens with eight KPI cards providing an immediate sales overview:

- **Total Revenue:** Aggregate sales revenue  
- **Total Orders:** Distinct count of transactions  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** Total units sold  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** Revenue per order  
- **Total Gross Profit:** Aggregate profitability  
- **Gross Margin (%):** Profitability relative to revenue  
- **Average Selling Price (ASP):** Revenue per unit sold  
- **Average Discount (%):** Average discount applied across orders  

These KPIs establish a top-level performance baseline before deeper exploration.

---

### 2. Interactive Slicers – Dynamic Filtering Controls

All visuals respond dynamically to the following slicers:

- **Date (Between Range Filter)**  
- **Product Category**  
- **Product Sub-Category**  
- **Sales Channel (Web / Mobile App)**  
- **Customer Segment (Prime / Non-Prime)**  
- **Country**

These controls allow business users to analyze performance across time, product hierarchy, channel, customer type, and geography.

---

### 3. Revenue Trend Analysis – Line Chart

**Revenue Trend Over Time – Line Chart**

- Axis: Date (Monthly Drill-Down)  
- Values: Total Revenue  

This visual highlights:

- Month-over-month revenue patterns  
- Seasonal fluctuations  
- Growth trajectory across 2021–2024  
- Filter-driven performance shifts  

It serves as the primary sales momentum indicator.

---

### 4. Product Performance Contribution

**Revenue by Product Category – Clustered Column Chart**

- Axis: Product Category  
- Values: Total Revenue  

This chart provides insight into:

- Category-level revenue contribution  
- High-performing and underperforming segments  
- Revenue concentration across product verticals  

It supports category strategy and commercial prioritization decisions.

---

### 5. Geographic Sales Distribution

**Revenue by Country – Map Visualization**

- Location: Customer Country  
- Size: Total Revenue  
- Tooltips: Total Orders, Gross Profit, AOV  

This visual enables:

- Geographic performance comparison  
- Market penetration analysis  
- Identification of revenue concentration by region  

It introduces a global commercial perspective.

---

### 6. Channel Revenue Mix

**Sales Channel Distribution – Donut Chart**

- Legend: Sales Channel  
- Values: Total Revenue  

This visual shows:

- Revenue split between Web and Mobile App  
- Channel dependency analysis  
- Digital channel performance comparison  

It supports channel optimization and digital strategy decisions.

## Tools & Data

- **Visualization Tool:** Power BI  
- **Dataset:** Fully synthetic global e-commerce dataset  
- **Record Count:** 100,000 transaction-level records
